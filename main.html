Summary

The Shawshank Redemption follows Andy Dufresne, a banker wrongly convicted of murdering his wife and her lover. Sentenced to life in Shawshank State Penitentiary, Andy forms an unlikely friendship with fellow inmate Red. Over the decades, Andy maintains hope through his intelligence and resourcefulness, eventually orchestrating a daring escape while exposing the prison's corruption. The story is a powerful exploration of hope, resilience, and the human spirit.

Actors

  • Tim Robbins as Andy Dufresne - A brilliant performance of a quiet, determined man
  • Morgan Freeman as Ellis "Red" Redding - The soulful narrator and heart of the film
  • Bob Gunton as Warden Norton - A chilling portrayal of corruption and hypocrisy
  • William Sadler as Heywood - Adding depth to the prison ensemble

Favorite Quote

"Get busy living or get busy dying." - Andy Dufresne

This powerful line encapsulates the film's central theme of choosing hope over despair, delivered with perfect conviction by Tim Robbins.
