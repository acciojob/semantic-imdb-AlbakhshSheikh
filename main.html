Summary

The Shawshank Redemption follows the story of Andy Dufresne, a banker wrongly convicted of murdering his wife and her lover. Sentenced to life in Shawshank State Penitentiary, Andy forms an unlikely friendship with fellow inmate Red. Over the decades, Andy maintains hope and secretly plans his escape while helping others find redemption within the prison walls.

Actors

  • Tim Robbins as Andy Dufresne - The determined protagonist
  • Morgan Freeman as Ellis Boyd "Red" Redding - The wise narrator
  • Bob Gunton as Warden Norton - The corrupt antagonist
  • William Sadler as Heywood - A fellow inmate
